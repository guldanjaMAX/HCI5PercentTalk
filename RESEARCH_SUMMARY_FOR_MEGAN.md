### WHAT MAKES THIS DIFFERENT FROM EXISTING TOOLS

**vs. Thomas-Kilmann (TKI):**
- TKI: Individual conflict *style* assessment
- HCI: Organizational *readiness for high-conflict people* assessment
- Positioning: Complementary, not competitive

**vs. Generic Conflict Training:**
- Training teaches techniques that work with reasonable people
- HCI identifies personalities where standard techniques escalate the situation
- Positioning: Second layer (specialized + systemic)

**vs. Change Management Readiness Tools:**
- Change tools assess initiative implementation
- HCI assesses organizational capability for *difficult people*
- Positioning: Different lens on organizational maturity
