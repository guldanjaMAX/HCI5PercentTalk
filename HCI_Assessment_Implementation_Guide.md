## SCORING ALGORITHM

### Step 1: Sum Points
Add all 10 responses: **Total Score = __ (range: 10–40)**

### Step 2: Convert to Percentage
```
Percentage Score = (Total Score - 10) / 30 × 100

Examples:
- Score 10 = 0%
- Score 17.5 = 25%
- Score 25 = 50%
- Score 32.5 = 75%
- Score 40 = 100%
```
