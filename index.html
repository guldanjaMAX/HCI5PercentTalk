The Hidden Cost

Every Organization Has Them

In every workplace, roughly 5% of people generate more than half of the complaints, grievances, and HR escalations. Not because they're having a bad month. Because they operate with a predictable, recognizable pattern that your current systems weren't designed to handle.

Your best managers are spending their most valuable hours managing your most difficult people. Your standard tools, open door policies, mediation, performance reviews, assume rational actors operating in good faith. High conflict personalities operate from a fundamentally different system.

What Your Audience Will Learn

A Talk That Teaches, Not Sells

This isn't a motivational speech with a pitch at the end. Your audience walks away with immediately usable tools they can apply the same afternoon. In 50 minutes, they'll learn to identify the pattern, de-escalate in real time, and understand why their current approaches aren't working.

10min

The Cost

Open with compelling data and a real story that makes the problem feel urgent and expensive. Every leader in the room will recognize their own version of the 5%.

15min

The Pattern

Teach the four characteristics of high conflict personalities: preoccupation with blame, all or nothing thinking, unmanaged emotions, and extreme behaviors. This is the moment the audience says "that's exactly what I'm dealing with."

15min

The Skill

Teach the BIFF Response method completely: Brief, Informative, Friendly, Firm. The audience practices rewriting real examples. They leave with a tool they can use that afternoon.

5min

The Vision

Paint the picture of a Conflict Smart organization. Share the transformation story: one healthcare system reduced HR complaints by 40% and dropped turnover from 38% to 12%.

5min

The Invitation

No pitch. Offer three paths: a free Conflict Readiness Assessment for everyone, a no pressure discovery call for the curious, and annual partnership programs for decision makers ready to move.
