## THE 10 QUESTIONS (4-Point Likert Scale)

| # | Dimension | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|-----------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | High-Conflict ID | Managers can identify high-conflict behaviors (blame, binary thinking, emotion dysregulation, extremity) | HCI's core differentiator |
| 2 | Leadership Capability | Senior leaders trained in de-escalation/BIFF frameworks | Leaders model behavior; critical |
| 3 | Formal Process | Documented, communicated conflict resolution procedure (peer→HR→mediation→action) | 72% lack this; foundational |
| 4 | Manager Training | Majority of managers trained in past 24 months | 60% gap nationally; critical gap |
| 5 | Psych Safety | Employees feel safe raising concerns without fear | Surfaces conflicts early vs. hiding |
| 6 | Escalation Recognition | Can recognize when conflict needs HR/mediation/escalation | Prevents wrong intervention |
| 7 | Employee Support | Resources in place (EAP, team coaching, post-conflict repair) | $3,600/employee/year absenteeism cost |
| 8 | HCP Protocols | Specific strategies for managing persistently difficult people | Most orgs apply generic discipline (escalates) |
| 9 | Policy & Documentation | Clear policies, consistent enforcement, documented consequences | Legal protection; 72% lack this |
| 10 | Strategic Commitment | Organization invested resources in capability over past 12 months | Indicates seriousness |
